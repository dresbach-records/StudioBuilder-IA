Senior Backend Engineer

Join our core systems team to build the high-performance infrastructure powering the next generation of AI-driven software development.

About StudioBuilder AI

StudioBuilder AI is a high-end tech firm specializing in advanced software development and systems engineering. We don't just build apps; we build the tools that build apps. Our mission is to bridge the gap between human creativity and machine efficiency through cutting-edge compilers, distributed systems, and real-time collaborative environments.

The Role

As a Senior Backend Engineer, you will be responsible for designing and implementing the core services that manage our massive-scale AI training pipelines and real-time synchronization engines. You'll work closely with our research and product teams to ensure our systems are robust, scalable, and extremely low-latency.

Key Responsibilities

  • check_circle Design and implement scalable microservices using Go and Rust.
  • check_circle Optimize database performance for high-concurrency real-time data.
  • check_circle Lead architectural discussions and perform deep code reviews.
  • check_circle Collaborate with SREs to ensure 99.99% system availability.

What We're Looking For

• 6+ years of professional experience in backend development.

• Strong proficiency in Go, Rust, or C++ with a focus on systems programming.

• Deep understanding of distributed systems, CAP theorem, and consensus algorithms (Raft/Paxos).

• Experience with Kafka, Redis, and PostgreSQL at scale.

• A passion for building tools that empower other developers.
